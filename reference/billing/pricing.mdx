Narrative offers two pricing models to fit different usage patterns and commitment levels.

## Pricing models

| Model | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Pay-as-you-go** | Use Narrative's Data Collaboration Platform with no up-front costs or minimum commitments |
| **Committed-Usage** | Lock in discounted usage rates when you commit to a set amount of platform usage over a period of time |

<Note>
Contact our sales team to discuss usage rate discounts for Committed-Usage plans.
</Note>

---

## Service fees

| Service | Pay-as-you-go | Committed-Usage |
|---------|---------------|-----------------|
| **Processing** | $0.65/GB | varies |
| **Transfer** | $1.50/GB | varies |
| **Transaction Services Fee** | 25% | varies |
| **Professional Services** | $150/hr | varies |

---

## Fee details

### Processing

Processing fees are based on the bytes processed per month during your usage of Narrative's products and services, including:

- Ingestion
- Evaluation
- Transactions on the Narrative Marketplace or private transactions
- Forecasting
- Order management

<Note>
**PAYG waiver for data transactions:** Pay-as-you-go Processing Fees are waived for activity directly supporting data transactions where you are offering data to be licensed or evaluated by a third-party, provided that the transaction (i) occurs or is expected to occur within 90 days of such activity, and (ii) is subject to Transaction Services Fees.
</Note>

### Transfer (Egress)

Transfer fees are calculated based on the volume of bytes transferred out of your Narrative account per month.

<Tip>
Egress Transfer to client-owned locations within AWS Region "us-east-1" (US East, N. Virginia) does not incur Transfer Fees.
</Tip>

### Transaction Services Fee

The Transaction Services Fee is a percentage of the [Licensed Data Fees](#licensed-data-fees) you transact, source, or facilitate on the platform or via Narrative Services, including via any Connector App.

<Note>
Narrative neither buys nor sells data. We provide software that powers commercial data transactions between companies. Fees for the licensing of data (Licensed Data Fees) are set by and paid to Data Licensors through Narrative's software.
</Note>

### Professional Services

Optional professional support services are available at an hourly rate.

---

## Usage calculation

Processing and Transfer are calculated in binary gigabytes (GB):

| Unit | Definition |
|------|------------|
| 1 GB | 2^30 bytes (1,073,741,824 bytes) |
| 1 TB | 2^40 bytes (1,024 GB) |

---

## Glossary

### Licensed Data Fees

Fees paid to Data Licensors for the licensing of data, set by and paid to the licensor through Narrative's software.
